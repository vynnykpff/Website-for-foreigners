Our partners
The International Association of Universities (IAU) is a membership-led non-governmental organization working in the field of higher education. It comprises more than 600 higher education institutions and organizations in over 130 countries. IAU is an official partner of UNESCO. The IAU secretariat is based in Paris and is located at the headquarters of UNESCO.

Polish: Akademia WSB, Silesia is a state-recognized private university, located in Dąbrowa Górnicza in Poland.It educates students, conducts research and co-operates to develop the economy. The university enrolls about 8 000 students supported by 200 employees (academic staff 180).


WSB University has international accreditations and memberships: CEEMAN IQA - in the field of managerial education awarded by the International Association for Management Development in Dynamic Societies CEEMAN, two accreditations EUR-ACE label for first-cycle studies in the field of IT.


And for first- and second-cycle studies in the field of Transport, awarded by the Accreditation Committee of Technical Universities (KAUT), Excellence in Research (LOGO HR) is awarded by the European Commission, which is aimed at increasing the attractiveness of working conditions and career development of scientists in the European Union, membership in The European University Association (EUA), as well as membership in the international association European Foundation for Management Development (EFMD) that promotes and enhances excellence in management development.

Turība University (Latvian: Biznesa augstskola „Turība”; formerly known as the Turība School of Business Administration) is the largest business school in Latvia. The university was established in 1993.


The university consists of four faculties with main study directions – business administration, law, international tourism, and communication. It runs a total of 25 study programmes, which include Professional Bachelors Program, Professional Masters Program and Doctoral study program.
