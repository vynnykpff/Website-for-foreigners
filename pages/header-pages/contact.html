International Educational and Training Center
International educational activity of the European University is aimed at ensuring its cooperation with the foreign university community, facilitating the processes of European integration, forming mechanisms of educational mobility, holding international scientific conferences, other academic events, providing educational services to foreigners. For this purpose, an International Educational and Training Center was established at the European University in 2008.
