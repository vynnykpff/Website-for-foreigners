Among various private institutions of higher education, the European university is one among the first and most popular. Over 30 years of its activity university has won considerable authority in educational and business spheres. Today the European University is modern and multidiscipline institution of higher education. Tens of thousands of future specialists are trained here on different forms of education. University graduates occupy top positions in various enterprises, companies and organizations both in Ukraine and abroad. European university was founded in 1991 by Timoshenko family.
