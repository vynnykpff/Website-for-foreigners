-1- What documents does required for an invitation to study?
  • Scanned copy of the original documents about previous education (if the documents do not have an English version, a translation into Ukrainian or English is mandatory)
  • Scanned copy of the original of the foreigner's international passport
  • Scanned copy of the consent to the processing of personal data
-2- How to get a student visa?
  • A visa is a stamp in a passport that certifies a foreigner's or stateless person's right to entry into Ukraine. Student visa (type D 13) does grant to persons who enter Ukraine for education ( pupils, students, graduate students, interns )
  • After receiving an invitation from the "European University" with the original invitation, it is necessary to contact the Embassy of Ukraine in your own or a neighboring country with an application for the issuance of an entry visa for studying in Ukraine
  • For receiving type D visas for the candidate for study, it is necessary to submit documents according to the list specified on the website of the Embassy
  • In addition, we draw your attention to the fact that documents about the student's previous education must be officially legalized in the original country or certified by an Apostille stamp before submitting to the consulate.
  • Tsi and documents must be formally recognized in Ukraine for the Ukrainian university could issue you a diploma after the end of training Click me
-3- What is "visa support"?
  • The Ukrainian State Center for International Education of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine performs registration of all Ukrainian invitations.
  • It provides official confirmation of such registration (visa support) to the Consulate of Ukraine in the relevant country.
  • Consulates only accept documents from international students with such registration and, therefore, cannot continue the procedure for issuing a student visa.
  • Visa support is a paid service of the Ukrainian State Center for International Education of the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine.
-4- Does the applicant need to inform the university about his arrival in Ukraine?
  • Necessarily. Having decided on the date of arrival in Ukraine, it is necessary to provide information to the International Educational and Training Center of the European University about the date of arrival and the flight number of the plane in order to ensure a meeting at the airport.
  • This will simplify the crossing procedure State border of Ukraine.

-8- A temporary permit residence?
Temporary permit residence — this document certifies the identity of a foreigner or a stateless person and confirms legal grounds for temporary residence in Ukraine. Documents for registration certificates are served personally to a foreigner to a state enterprise that belongs to the field management of the SMS or of the territorial body of the State Internal Revenue Service by location registration.
For temporary registration permits, the residence is provided such the documents:`
  • Statement a foreigner
  • Passport document of a foreigner or document that certifies a stateless person ( after presentation returns ), with a type D visa, if nothing else is provided legislation and international treaties of Ukraine, and copies passport pages with personal data and visa.
  • Translation into Ukrainian language pages of a foreigner's passport document or a document that certifies a stateless person, certified in the established legislation of order.
  • A receipt for the payment of state duty or a document which confirms the availability benefits of his payment.
  • Request ( submission ) of the university of design foreigners or a stateless person temporary permits residence
