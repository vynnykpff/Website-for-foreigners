Admission and study conditions
  • The term of study is 10 months.
The program of the preparatory department includes:
  • Studying the Ukrainian or English language.
  • Study of the basic disciplines of a comprehensive school.
  • Certificate of completion of preparatory department
    • ×
Cost of education:
  • Tuition - 1100 USD.
  • Tuition + accommodation in a dormitory - 1500 USD.
  • Online (distance) education – 700 USD.
To enroll in the Preparatory Department, the applicant must submit the following documents to the Admissions Committee of the University:
  • The original document on previous education.
  • A copy of an international passport, a copy of documents on previous education with a notarized translation into Ukrainian.
  • 8 photo cards of size 3.5x4.5.
Upon successful completion of the Preparatory Department, foreign citizens receive an official certificate. With which graduates of the Preparatory Department can also enter other higher educational institutions of Ukraine.
Foreign citizens who completed a training course at the Preparatory Department and did not pass the exams can enroll for repeated training
